Welcome to traile! Here's how to play:
You're given two countries on the globe. You have to name countries that form a path between them.
You don't have to name the whole path at once; just name countries individually.
Use the globe controls to explore and find the countries. Complete the path to move on to the next challenge.
Good luck and enjoy exploring the world!
